About Me

Meng Liu is a Ph.D. candidate supervised by Prof. Xinwang Liu at National University of Defense Technology. He is also a Joint Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Beng Chin OOI at National University of Singapore. His research interests include graph neural network and clustering. He has published more than 20 papers as including TPAMI, TKDE, Adv Sci, CVPR, ICLR, and AAAI, with 1 ESI Hot Paper, 2 ESI Highly Cited Paper, and 900 citations. He received awards such as Best Paper of 2024 China Computational Power Conference, Best Student Paper of CCHI 2023, Excellent Poster of 2024 World Young Scientist Summit, three China National Scholarships, CSC Scholarship, Outstanding Master Thesis, etc. He is on the (Youth) Editorial Board of INSC, JAET, DLPR. He also serves as Area Chair for COLM, IJCNN, ICIC, and reviewer for TKDE, TOIS, TNNLS, NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, etc.

I am looking for potential research positions, please feel free to contact me.
