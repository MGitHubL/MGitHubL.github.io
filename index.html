About Me

Meng Liu is a Ph.D student advised by Prof. Xinwang Liu at National University of Defense Technology. His research interests include Temporal Graph Learning, Deep Clustering, and Knowledge Graph. He has published 6 papers as first author including SIGIR, ACM MM, and CIKM, which have received more than 200 citations. He received awards such as China National Scholarships (Twice), Best Student Paper of CCHI 2023, Excellent Master Thesis of Heilongjiang University, etc. He also serves as reviewer for TKDE, TOIS, TNNLS, TOMM, NeurIPS, AAAI, and ACM MM, etc.

Email: mengliuedu@163.com / mengliu@nudt.edu.cn
