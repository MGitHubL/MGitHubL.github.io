About Me

Meng Liu is a Ph.D student at National University of Defense Technology, since 2022, was advised by Prof. Xinwang Liu (PRMI Group). His research interests include Temporal Graph Learning and Deep Clustering. He has published 10+ papers in conferences and journals, including SIGIR, ACM MM, CIKM and BIB, etc. He also serves as reviewer for conferences and journals, including NeurIPS, AAAI, ACM MM, WSDM, CIKM, and TNNLS, PR, TOMM, etc.
